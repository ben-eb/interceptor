Intel (Escape to go back)

Controls

  • Z - Fire main cannon.
  • X - Deploy smart bomb.
  • Arrow keys - Pilot the ship.
  • Escape - Pause.

Player Ship

An agile spacecraft that features a fast firing, front mounted cannon and capacity for three bombs. The perfect weapon for taking out multiple waves of enemy drones.

Enemy Drones

Plasma Turret

Stationary emplacement weapon carrying twin plasma cannons that fire directly at the player.

:TP:Drone

Large drone that rapidly teleports from one side of the screen to the other.

Orbital Reconnaissance Bot

Small bot that orbits other, larger drones. Has no weapons of its own.

Z133 Drone

A fast moving drone that homes in towards nearby heat signatures. Limited fuel capacity.

DDrone

Your regular old mass market drone. Cost cutting meant that these drones have no weapons, but appear in large numbers.

DDrone MKII

Due to increasing complaints about the non-lethality of the regular model, the MKII has an onboard laser cannon. It fires in one direction.

RecoII

When destroyed, shoots out a small bullet burst.
